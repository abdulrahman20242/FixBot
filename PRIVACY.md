# FixBot Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** September 29, 2025

## Introduction

FixBot is a Discord support assistant designed to help members of the Command & Conquer: Generals Zero Hour community by providing AI-powered troubleshooting and support directly within Discord.

By interacting with FixBot, you agree to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## What Information We Process

FixBot only processes the content of messages when a user intentionally mentions the bot and requests assistance.

Examples include:

* Technical support questions.
* Troubleshooting game errors.
* Requests for guidance related to Command & Conquer: Generals Zero Hour.
* General support inquiries.

FixBot does **not** monitor or process all conversations within a Discord server.

## How Your Data Is Used

When you mention FixBot, the content of your message is forwarded to the Z.ai Agent service to generate a response and provide support.

The information is used solely to:

* Understand your request.
* Generate relevant responses.
* Maintain conversation context when necessary.
* Improve support efficiency for the community.

## Third-Party Services

FixBot relies on the Z.ai platform to provide AI-generated responses.

As part of this integration:

* Message content sent to FixBot may be transmitted to and processed by Z.ai.
* Conversation history may be retained by Z.ai according to their policies.
* Z.ai may use submitted data to improve and develop its services and AI models, as described in their Privacy Policy.

For more information, please review the Z.ai Privacy Policy:
https://chat.z.ai/privacy-policy

## Data Storage

FixBot itself does not independently store message content.

However, messages processed through the Z.ai integration may be retained by Z.ai in accordance with their Privacy Policy and operational requirements.

## User Choice

Using FixBot is entirely voluntary.

Users choose whether to interact with the bot by mentioning it directly. If you do not wish for your message content to be processed through the Z.ai integration, please do not mention or interact with FixBot.

## Children's Privacy

FixBot is not intended for individuals under the age required by Discord's Terms of Service.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or FixBot's data practices, please contact the bot administrator through the associated Discord server.
